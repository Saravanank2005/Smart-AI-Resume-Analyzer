Role: Product Manager
Category: Product Management

Core Skills:
- Product Strategy & Vision: Defining product roadmaps, market analysis, competitor analysis, business models
- Methodology: Agile, Scrum, Kanban, User Story mapping, PRDs (Product Requirement Documents)
- Analytics & Metrics: North Star Metric, KPI definition, SQL, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Google Analytics
- User Focus: User research, customer interviewing, persona definition, wireframing, usability testing
- Leadership & Communication: Stakeholder alignment, cross-functional collaboration, prioritization (RICE, MoSCoW)

Key Responsibilities:
- Define the product vision, strategy, and roadmap based on market opportunities and user feedback.
- Write detailed PRDs, user stories, and acceptance criteria for engineering and design teams.
- Prioritize features and balance technical debt, user requests, and business goals.
- Collaborate closely with engineers, designers, marketing, and sales to launch high-quality products.
- Analyze post-launch product metrics and user behavior to iterate and improve the product.

Standard Tools & Technologies:
- Management: Jira, Confluence, Asana, Monday.com
- Analytics: Amplitude, Mixpanel, SQL, Google Analytics
- Design/Wireframing: Figma, Balsamiq, Miro
- Roadmap: Productboard, Roadmunk

Typical Interview Focus Areas:
- Product Design (improving a favorite product, designing a product for a specific niche)
- Product Strategy (market entry, monetization, response to competitor moves)
- Analytics & Metrics (troubleshooting a declining metric, choosing launch metrics)
- Prioritization & Trade-offs (handling conflicting priorities from engineering vs sales)
- Behavioral & Leadership (handling failure, managing difficult stakeholders, cross-functional influence)
